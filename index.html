Jun "NUJABES" Seba

Godfather of lo-fi hip-hop.

A time line of Nujabes' life:

  • 1974 - Born in Minato City, Japan.
  • 1995 - Opens his first record store in Shibuya named "Guinness Records" at the age of 21.
  • 1998 - Founded the independent record label, "Hydeout Productions".
  • 1999 - Releases his first 12-inch record, "Ain't No Mystery".
  • 2003 - Releases his first full length album, "Metaphorical Music".
  • 2004 - Gains international recognition with the anime "Samurai Champloo", which he made the music for.
  • 2005 - Releases sophmore album, "Modal Soul".
  • 2010 - On February 26 Seba was hit in a traffic collision. He would be pronounced dead at the hospital.

Through his soulful music, Nujabes has touched so many people around the world, even beyond his dreams. He was a mysterious character to most as he avoided the public limelight... Yet it continued to amaze me how young listeners of all backgrounds learned of his enigmatic name, and expressed support for his music.

-- Shing02
